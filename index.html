// ABOUT THE ARTIST
PROCESS

I work across digital, print, and physical installation — treating each medium not as a constraint but as a set of rules to break intentionally.

My practice is grounded in systems thinking: what happens when you subject any system to extreme conditions? Entropy, degradation, and structural failure interest me as much as polish.

I believe the best design is slightly wrong on purpose.
